Partner FAQ

Coverage Details And Common Questions

These answers help search engines and buyers quickly understand how the Fred's Auto Removal partner program works across local and national markets.

Who should apply for this partner program?

Salvage yards, junk car buyers, dismantlers, auto recyclers, towing operators, scrap buyers, and vehicle acquisition teams can all apply.

Can I choose the areas I want to cover?

Yes. You can share ZIP codes, cities, counties, metro areas, or full states so coverage matches your real service area.

What vehicle types can I request?

You can outline preferences for non-runners, wrecked vehicles, older cars, salvage units, scrap vehicles, and other inventory types.

How fast will Fred's contact me after I apply?

A member of the partner team will review your application and typically follow up within one business day to discuss next steps.
